Program Information

Welcome to our Free Rock Climbing for Kids program! We are excited to offer a fun and adventurous experience for children to learn and explore the world of rock climbing. This program is designed to provide kids with a safe and supportive environment to develop their physical and mental skills while having a great time.

Whether your child is a beginner or has previous climbing experience, our dedicated team of instructors is here to guide them through the journey. We focus on teaching proper climbing techniques, safety protocols, and fostering a sense of teamwork and accomplishment.
