Program Information

Rock Cancer is an inspiring initiative that offers free rock climbing sessions to young patients aged 4-25 battling cancer or with a history of the ailment, along with other chronic medical conditions. Hosted every third Friday at Planet Rock Ann Arbor, this program empowers participants to reclaim their strength, enhance their range of motion, and rebuild confidence post-treatment. Additionally, each participant can bring along a friend or sibling, fostering a sense of community and mutual support. Beyond climbing, Rock Cancer serves as a beacon of hope and resilience, celebrating the indomitable spirit of its young climbers.
