What is the Saint Andrew's Breakfast Program

In the heart of Ann Arbor, MI, the Breakfast Program at Saint Andrew's is more than just a place to grab a bite. Since 2021, I have had the honor of serving as a Food Server at this initiative. Recognizing the power of nutrition as a cornerstone of preventative medicine, we not only provide our city's underserved community with wholesome breakfasts but also offer clothing and prepared nutritional bagged lunches. Our goal goes beyond merely feeding: we aim to foster well-being, empowerment, and hope through each meal and interaction. At Saint Andrew's, we believe that when you feed the body with care, you nourish the soul and empower the spirit.
